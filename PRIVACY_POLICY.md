# Privacy Policy (Политика конфиденциальности)

## CheckTracker - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 28, 2025**

### Overview

CheckTracker is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application.

### Data Collection and Storage

#### What We Collect
- Receipt text (raw OCR output)
- Expense data (amount, merchant, category, date)
- User preferences (theme, currency, language)

#### How We Store It
- **All data is stored locally on your device only**
- No data is sent to cloud servers
- No personal information is collected
- No tracking or analytics cookies

### Your Rights

- ✅ **Full Control**: You can view, edit, or delete any expense record at any time
- ✅ **No Synchronization**: Your data never leaves your device
- ✅ **No Third Parties**: We don't share data with any external services
- ✅ **No Advertising**: The app contains no ads or tracking

### Permissions

The app requires:
- **Camera**: For scanning receipts via OCR
- **Storage**: For temporary caching of images (cache is cleared automatically)

We will always ask before accessing your camera or storage.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

### Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
